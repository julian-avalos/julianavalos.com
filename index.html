About Me

I help clients make their vision a reality, helping them from ideation to implementation.

I enjoy working both in the front- and back-end side of web development. I remember my introduction to web development, if you could call it that, was modifying MySpace profile themes.

That feeling of finally making something look the way I wanted it was addicting, and I decided I wanted to pursue that in a professional manner by becoming a web developer. I still get that same feeling when I finally get a feature to work the way I envisioned it, when I present my work to a client and they love it, or when I see a project I've worked on go live. It's a great feeling, and one I hope to continue experiencing throughout my career.

Feel free to reach out if you have any questions or would like to work together!
